    Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom · right-drag to pan. The SVGs are real-millimetre cut files (hairline red strokes), one file per sheet, kerf-compensated so the joints press-fit. Glue each inner side layer to its outer layer (the lamination hides the lid grooves), assemble, and the lid slides dry — never glue it to the box. The lid frame glues onto the lid only: slide the lid in first and centre the frame in its recess (front edges flush) so the recess jigs it. The preview and the cut paths come from the same outlines, so the box matches what you see.
